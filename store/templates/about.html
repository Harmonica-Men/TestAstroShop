About Us

Welcome to the Astroshop. In our Astroshop, you will find the largest range of astronomical products in Belgium at very competitive prices. The range consists of more than 1000 items.

Whether it is a book, atlas, globe, weather station, or toy, we have it all. For optical material, we are a distributor of the brands Omegon, Meade, Bresser, Celestron, and Sky-Watcher. This allows us to also offer telescopes, binoculars, eyepieces, and other accessories, for children, beginners, and advanced users.

Can't find what you are looking for on our website? The complete range of optics from the different brands that we distribute are available to order.

Our physical store is located at 'Volkssterrenwacht Hove' in the reception area.
